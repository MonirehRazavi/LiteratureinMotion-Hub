Group 1 - Diasporic Canadian Literature from the Global South

Subject:
Circulation of authorship into Canada

Topic:
Works published or originating in Canada written by authors who are citizens of or were born in Africa, South America, Asia, Europe, Oceania, Central America, and the Caribbean.

Goal:
To explore which immigrants get published in Canada.

SPARQL Query:
SELECT DISTINCT
?author
?authorLabel
?work
?workTitle

WHERE { # query without Europe/Oceania gave 194 results, including them gave 853 results.
  ?work wdt:P50 ?author . # person is author
  ?author wdt:P27 ?nationality . # author's nationality
  ?author wdt:P19 ?pob # author's place of birth
  {?nationality wdt:P30 wd:Q48} UNION {?pob wdt:P30 wd:Q48} # nationality or place of birth in Asia
  UNION
  {?nationality wdt:P30 wd:Q18} UNION {?pob wdt:P30 wd:Q18} # nationality or place of birth in South America
  UNION
  {?nationality wdt:P30 wd:Q15} UNION {?pob wdt:P30 wd:Q15} # nationality or place of birth in Africa
  UNION
  {?nationality wdt:P30 wd:Q46} UNION {?pob wdt:P30 wd:Q46} # nationality or place of birth in Europe
  UNION
  {?nationality wdt:P30 wd:Q55643} UNION {?pob wdt:P30 wd:Q55643} # nationality or place of birth in Oceania
  UNION
  {?nationality wdt:P361 wd:Q27611} UNION {?pob wdt:P361 wd:Q27611} # nationality or place of birth in Central America
  UNION
  {?nationality wdt:P361 wd:Q664609} UNION {?pob wdt:P361 wd:Q664609} . # nationality or place of birth in the Caribbean
  
  {?work wdt:P495 wd:Q16} # work originated in Canada
  UNION
  {?work wdt:P291 wd:Q16} # work published in Canada
  UNION
  {?work wdt:P291 [wdt:P17 wd:Q16]} . # work published in a province in Canada
  
  OPTIONAL {?work wdt:P1476 ?workTitle}
  SERVICE wikibase:label { bd:serviceParam wikibase:language "en". }
  }
